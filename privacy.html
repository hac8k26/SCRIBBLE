Privacy Policy

LAST_UPDATED · MARCH 2026

// 01 · WHAT_WE_COLLECT

When you create an account, we collect your name, age, college, email address, and any bio or profile photo you choose to provide.

We also store messages you send to other users and records of profiles you have liked or matched with.

// 02 · HOW_WE_USE_IT

  • To show your profile to other logged-in users on the browse page.
  • To enable messaging between matched users.
  • To send you in-app notifications about likes and messages.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.

// 03 · DATA_STORAGE

Your data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) hosted in the EU region. Profile images are stored in Supabase Storage. All connections are encrypted via HTTPS.

// 04 · YOUR_RIGHTS

  • You can edit your profile at any time from the profile page.
  • You can delete your account and all associated data permanently from the profile page.
  • You can unmatch and delete conversations at any time.

// 05 · OPEN_SOURCE

NAPKIN is open source under the MIT license. You can inspect the full codebase on GitHub. We have no hidden data collection beyond what is described here.

// 06 · CONTACT

Questions? Open an issue on our GitHub repository.
