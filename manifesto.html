OURMANIFESTO.

What we believe, why we built this, and how we plan to keep it real.

Scroll down ↓
01
#01

Radical Transparency

Every line of code that powers your connections is public. No black-box algorithms deciding your worth. No hidden scoring. You can read, fork, and audit everything we do.

02
#02

People Over Profit

We don't sell your data. We don't run engagement-bait dark patterns. We don't gamify loneliness. SCRIBBLE exists to help people connect — full stop.

03
#03

Community Ownership

This platform belongs to its users. Features are voted on, bugs are fixed together, and the roadmap is public. If you don't like something, open a PR.

04
#04

Safety by Design

Moderation tools are built-in, not bolted on. Consent is a first-class feature. We take harassment seriously and the community helps enforce it.

05
#05

Free, Forever

Core features will always be free. No paywalled matches. No "premium" visibility boosts. Love shouldn't cost a subscription.

— The Scribble Community ✍️
