Community Guidelines

LAST_UPDATED · MARCH 2026

// THE_BASICS

NAPKIN is built for honest, respectful connections between college students. These guidelines exist to keep it that way. They are short, clear, and non-negotiable.

// THE_RULES

01
BE_REAL
Use your actual name, real age, and a genuine photo. No fake profiles, no catfishing.
02
BE_RESPECTFUL
Treat every person on this platform the way you'd want to be treated. No harassment, no hate speech, no unsolicited explicit content.
03
NO_SPAM
Don't send copy-paste messages to everyone. Genuine conversation only.
04
KEEP_IT_SAFE
Don't share personal contact details (phone numbers, addresses) until you trust someone. Meet in public places first.
05
ONE_ACCOUNT
One person, one account. Duplicate or bot accounts will be removed.

// DO_AND_DONT

✓ DO
  • Be honest about who you are
  • Start conversations with genuine interest
  • Respect when someone doesn't reply
  • Report profiles that seem fake
  • Unmatch if you're not interested
✗ DON'T
  • Use someone else's photos
  • Send threatening or abusive messages
  • Pressure anyone for anything
  • Share other users' private info
  • Use the platform for commercial purposes

// ENFORCEMENT

Violations of these guidelines may result in account suspension or permanent removal. Since NAPKIN is open source, the community helps maintain standards. If you encounter a profile or message that violates these guidelines, unmatch and report it via GitHub.
