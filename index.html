Why Kindred

Dating apps are broken.
We're fixing that.

🔍

Transparent Matching

Our matching logic lives on GitHub. Read it, audit it, improve it. No black box deciding who you meet.

🗝️

You Own Your Data

Export everything, delete everything, self-host everything. Your data never leaves without your permission.

🌱

Intentional Connections

No infinite scroll. A curated set of compatible people per week. Quality over dopamine hits.

The Process

Simple by design.

01

Build your profile

Tell us who you are — your values, interests, and what you're genuinely looking for.

02

Get matched

Our open algorithm surfaces a handful of compatible people. No swiping, no gamification.

03

Start talking

Reach out when you're ready. No timers, no pressure, no expiring matches.

04

Meet in real life

The app is a bridge, not a destination. We celebrate when you close the laptop.
